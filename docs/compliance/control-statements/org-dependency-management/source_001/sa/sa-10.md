# sa-10 - \[SA\] Developer Configuration Management

## Control Statement

Require the developer of the system, system component, or system service to perform configuration management during system, component, or service development, integration, and implementation.

## What is the solution and how is it implemented?

### org-dependency-management

#### Part c

The org mandates that all CI/CD pipeline dependencies be tracked, versioned, and updated through a controlled process (Renovate + PR review). The dependency lint check verifies compliance. SHA-pinned refs and lockfiles provide a complete manifest of external dependencies, ensuring all third-party components are tracked under configuration management.
